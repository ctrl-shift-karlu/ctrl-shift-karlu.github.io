Our Story

Back in 2021, during the height of the pandemic, we started Payag ni Parekoy with a simple goal—to keep going despite the challenges. While many businesses were struggling, we opened a small kubo serving takoyaki and milkshakes, hoping to bring comfort through affordable food.

The beginning was not easy. There were slow days, limited resources, and a lot of uncertainty about whether the business would survive. Still, we continued showing up every day, preparing each order with care and doing our best to improve little by little.

As time passed, more people started discovering our small payag. Customers shared their experiences with friends and on social media, and slowly, our humble food stall became a place where people gathered to enjoy good food and a warm atmosphere.

Because of the support we received, we eventually expanded into a bigger space and added more meals and food packages to our menu. Even as the business grew, we stayed committed to the same values we started with—serving food that is affordable, delicious, and made with passion.

Today, Payag ni Parekoy is more than just a food business to us. It is a reminder that even small beginnings can grow into something meaningful through hard work, perseverance, and the support of the community.
